About Me

I am a resident of San Leandro, CA, who recently just completed Full Stack Web Development program at UC Berkeley adding to my Bachelor's degree in Electronics Engineering. I have worked for a little over 20 years, mostly, in the tech industry. I worked at Intel for ten years straight out of college and recently, at Lawrence Livermore National Lab for three years.

I really wanted to tap into my creative side thus my investment in coding bootcamp to hopefully reignite the passion that I need, to feel inspired in a work place. And at this moment, I am loving every day of it! I hope to challenge myself to code everyday to hone my skills and find a job where I can learn from great mentors and feel supported and encouraged so I could be a mentor for others in the years ahead.

I am highly independent, adaptable, timely, a fast learner, hard worker and a doer. Constantly praised for excellent technical ability, organization and communication skills by supervisors, engineers and peers - skills that help me succeed in any job role. I am confident that these traits I possess will also help me succeed in this new endeavor I am going to take.
